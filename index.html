Stains

Red wine:
We’ve all been there..
Accidentally knocking down a glass on the table spilling the red wine all over the table cloth.
No worries we’ll fix this, your great grandma's table cloth will be just fine.

How do we fix this?
Rinse the table cloth under water and pour some salt on it, don’t be shy with the salt.
The salt will absorb the red wine, you could also use potato stash or bicarbonate.
After roughly 30 minutes scrape the salt off and the stain should be gone.
If it isn’t put it in the laundry machine and let that do the rest, this works every time for me!

Oil or fat:
This is my favourite and probably the easiest stain to fix.
Spilling oil on a piece of clothing is very common, maybe you did it when cooking or eating..
So how do we fix this without throwing your favourite shirt away?

How do we fix this?
We all have it in the kitchen, "washing up liquid".
This is fat repellant and will absorb most fat stains.
Pour some hot water on the stain and a lot of washing up liquid for you to scrub in.
Let sit for roughly 15 minutes and then rinse off.
The stain should be gone once the piece of clothing has dried.
One of the best brands to use for this is the Swedish brand “YES”.

Stearin:
Knocked over a candle and the stearin melts into the table cloth?
No worries all you have to do is put it in the freezer for a bit then scrape the stearin off.
Or you could use a coffee filter and a steamer.

How do we fix this?
Take out your steamer and put some coffee filters under and above the stain on your table cloth.
Use the steamer to melt the stearin all over again but this time the coffee filters will absorb the stearin instead.
You might have to change filter more often depending on the stain size.
Reglate the heat so no firer will occur.

Feeling sick ?

Coughing:
Coughing late in the night and can’t sleep, I know the feeling..
There is hope for you, just make sure you have 1 or 2 yellow onions at home.

How do we fix this?
Bring out a big bowl and start boiling some water in the background.
Take the onion, slice each onion about 4 times and put it in the bowl.
Pour a decent amount of hot water over the onion.
Leave the steamed onion next to your bed for when you go to bed.
It might smell a little bit but you will get that sleep back, it’ll be worth it.

The ginger is good for your immune system and will help to boost it back up again, allowing your body to defeat it a little bit faster.

I would only recommend it to people who use snus on regular basis, but it does take the pain away.
